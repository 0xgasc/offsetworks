The Model

      

        
One designer, no middlemen

        
Agencies charge for account managers, project managers, office space, and layers of process. Most of that doesn't improve the final product.

        
Offset strips that out. Clients talk directly to the person building their site. That means faster turnaround and lower prices.

        
Target market: startups and SMBs who need custom work but can't justify $30K+ agency fees. Sweet spot is $5-15K projects.

        
Geography: US (NYC, Miami) and LATAM (Guatemala, Mexico). Bilingual, strong networks in both markets.
